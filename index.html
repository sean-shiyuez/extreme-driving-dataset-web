Sensor Configuration

  • 5× Surround RGB cameras (front-left, front-right, rear-left, rear-right, rear-center)
  • ZED 2i Stereo camera — left + right RGB with per-frame dense depth maps (.npy)
  • LiDAR Forward-facing LiDAR point clouds (.npy) per frame
  • GNSS High-precision GPS: north/east velocity, absolute heading, centimeter-level positioning
  • Chassis Speed, yaw rate, raw IMU (X/Y acceleration) — direct side-slip angle derivation

Select a scene category to explore. Each episode contains fully synchronized multi-sensor streams — surround RGB, ZED stereo + depth, LiDAR, and vehicle dynamics — resampled to a uniform 4 Hz grid.

Normal Driving

Everyday scenarios: car-following, lane changes, turns, intersections, tunnels, and gradual deceleration. Reference driving and chassis signals.

Complex Traffic Driving

Dense traffic, ramps, overpasses, construction zones, yielding to pedestrians, and complex multi-lane intersections.

Critical Driving

Emergency braking, evasive maneuvers, obstacle avoidance, and near-collision limit-handling — the centerpiece of this release.

Low Light Driving

Night and underexposed driving conditions testing perception robustness under severely reduced visibility.

Rain Driving

Rainy weather with wet pavement reflections and reduced visibility, challenging sensor perception and braking distance.

Snow Driving

Snow-covered road surfaces with extreme low friction, testing traction control and tire–surface interaction under winter conditions.
